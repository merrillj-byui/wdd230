Overview

Site Name

Elk Ridge City Chamber of Commerce - This name represents the Chamber of Commerce operating within Elk Ridge City.

Purpose

The Elk Ridge City Chamber of Commerce functions to unify businesses operating within Elk Ridge City to support business interests and advocate for the city.

Audience

Businesses operating within Elk Ridge City, outside businesses wanting to engage with our businesses, and community members who are interested in activities sponsored by our businesses.

Scenarios

  • What current and upcoming events are sponsored or supported by the chamber?
  • How do I reach members of the chamber's board of directors?
  • What are the city demographics?
  • What are the current weather conditions?
  • Are there recent congratulations, callouts, or news?
  • Where is the chamber located and how do I contact them?
